The Crinkle Shop's First Opening!

The Crinkle Shop was created by a person who is passionate about the love of baking crinkles.

The purpose of The Crinkle Shop is to spread the feeling of eating the chocolate goodness and being able to explore other cookies around the world.

Country of Origin

Crinkles was originated from St.Paul, Minnesota. Throughout the century, it came to the Philippines and it became a well-known snack for Filipino children.

Crinkles are every Filipino's childhood snack. It is an affordable cookie that Filipino children can purchase.

The Crinkle Donation Association

For every purchase, it will donate 2% towards The Crinkle Donation Association!

The purpose of this association is to relieve low-income Filipino families in the Philippines from financial burden and be able to go to college with no financial problems.
